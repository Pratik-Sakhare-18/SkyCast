Who We Are

Sky Cast is a modern weather forecasting platform that provides accurate, real-time updates on temperature, air quality, UV index, sunrise & sunset, and much more. Our mission is to keep you prepared for every day by delivering reliable weather insights in a simple and elegant way.

Our Mission

We believe weather impacts every decision — from your morning walk to global business operations. That’s why Sky Cast combines science, technology, and user-friendly design to make weather data accessible to everyone
